## 外貌

A 17-year-old boy with short, messy black hair, wearing a high school uniform consisting of a white shirt, black blazer, and gray trousers. He has sharp, expressive eyes and a lean build.

## 性格

务实而坚定，但在与三叶交换身体之前，常常感到与周围环境格格不入。

## 背景

Taki is a high school student living in Tokyo. He works part-time at an Italian restaurant and has a passion for architecture. He is practical and determined, but often feels disconnected from his surroundings until he starts swapping bodies with Mitsuha.
